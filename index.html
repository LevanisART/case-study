OUR APPROACH

From the minute, Sweat Pants Agency was given the challenge, we immediately dove into all aspects of acquisition.

Facebook Advertising
With Facebook Advertising, we started by analyzing the top performing audiences and creatives over the last 90 days, completely rebuilding campaigns from scratch.
At the same time, we started to develop new copy and creative, utilizing existing assets and looking at available user generated content. Additionally, we dove into customer reviews, customer services log, and customer surveys to develop new advertising angles.
After seeing some improvement but wanting for more, the co-founding partner of Sweat Pants Agency (David Tendrich) hopped on the customers and found out that customers were using Hunt A Killer as a date night product (a way for couples to connect in an engaging actvity at home).
Once making this discovery, we rewrote the ads to lean into date night and saw a masive improvement in cost of acquisition while expanding our market.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)
While improving our Facebook ads, we also attacked conversion rate optimization with full force. We started by installing heatmaps and website recordings. We started by expanding credibility on the site, beefing up testimonials, and eliminating sections that didn't improve converison such as How It Works.
Then using customer feedback, we developed new hypotheses that we implemented in our CRO testing. We added a subtitle that reflected our top ad, saying "This Date Night, Hunt a Serial Killer Together". We added questions to our quiz funnel that asks people "How They Plan On Playing The Game" and much more.
Through all of these efforts, we were able to 2x Hunt A Killer's conversion over the next year.
