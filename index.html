CONVERSION RATE OPTIMIZATION

Over a year of working together we were able to 2x conversion

Injecting Social Proof

One of the enhancements we made was introducing more social proof and concepts of use. 1m+ episodes shipped creates a 'jump on the bandwagon' feel, while each of the individual UGC (user generated content) were carefully curated based on customer feedback into how they play the game: Home alone with their dog in their PJs... date night... meeting friends at the pub... cracking open a bottle of wine... etc.

Section Title

Hunt a Killer places immense detail into their games. Our hypothesis was if we showed that more strongly, we'd inspire more sales. And we were right. This section gave a nice lift on conversion and is still the control with multiple 8-figures of ad traffic encountering it.

Section Title

We discovered our audience had an affinity for Netflix, so we formed a new hypothesis: If we present the boxes that make up the full series as 'episodes' in a 'season' -- inspired by how Netflix presents episodes and summaries -- we'd see a lift. Again, we were right, and there's evidence this helped retention/churn as well as it introduced future pacing.
